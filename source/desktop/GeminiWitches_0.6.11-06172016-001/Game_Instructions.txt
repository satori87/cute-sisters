	***********************************
	* GEMINI WITICHES (Version 0.6.11) *
	***********************************

** A Platformer about two twin sisters! **
	
** Stats **
Green Bar = Health
Blue Bar = Magic (can't use elemental magic when it reaches 0)
Yellow Bar = Experience

** Controls **

F1 = Toggle Fullscreen
F5 = Reset Game
ESC = Exit Game

KeyBoard controls
------------------
<- = Left
-> = Right
^ =  Jump / Enter Door
<Enter> = Menu Selection / Pause
'S' Back in Menu

'A' Attack (Hold to Charge with Seika)
'Z' Flower of life
'W' Fly
'X' Piercing Water Crystal
'Q' Fire Burst

XBOX 360 controller controls
----------------------------
*Left Analog
<- = Left
-> = Right
'A' = Jump / Enter Door
<Start> = Menu Selection / Pause
'B' Back in Menu

'X' Attack (Hold to Charge Only Seika)

*While Holding Down RT (Right Back Trigger)
'Y' Flower of life
'A' Fly
'X' Piercing Water Crystal
'B' Fire Burst


** ELEMENTAL POWERS**

*Mirror Shot
-Shoots a small mirror energy shot.  Doesn't cost mp.  Charge for a more powerful attack.

**Collect all four elemental gems!

*Earth (orange gem)
-Boost max hp
-damages enemies when character lands on top of them.
-[Element Power] Flower of life - Restores health.

*Wind (green gem)
-Slight boost in speed.
-Double Jump
-[Element Power] Fly - Self explanory ;-)

*Fire (red gem)
-Physical attacks are twice as strong.
-[Element Power] Fire Burst - Fire attack that surrouds forming a type of rotating shield.

*Water (blue gem)
-Boosts max mp
-Boosts mp regen
-[Element Power] Piercing Water Crystal - Pierces through walls at super speeds to attack enemies!
